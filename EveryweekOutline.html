TITLE: SAVED AND THANKFUL

TEXT: LUKE 17:11-19

INTRO:
Many received God’s blessing, but only a few respond with thanksgiving.

OBJECTIVE:
To emphasize the relationship between salvation and thankfulness.

I - THE LEPERS' C _ _ _ _ _ _ _ N (17:11-13)

A) SEPARATION (17:11-12)
    (ISAIAH 59:2)

B) SUPPLICATION (17:13)
    (TITUS 3:5; HEBREWS 4:16)

II - THE LEPERS' C _ _ _ _ _ _ G (17:14-16)

A) FOLLOW (17:14)
    (JOHN 6:2; 1 JOHN 1:9)

B) FAITH (17:15-16)
    (2 CORINTHIANS 5:7; LUKE 11:28)

III - THE LEPER'S C _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ N (17:17-19)

A) THE LORD’S EXPECTATION (17:17-18)
    (LUKE 8:13; ROMANS 1:21)

B) THE LORD’S ENDORSEMENT (17:19)
    (JOHN 1:12-13)

CONCLUSION:
10 men were cleansed but only one changed.
10 were healed but only one was saved.
10 received mercy but only one remained true and grateful.
