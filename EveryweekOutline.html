TITLE: FROM DOUBT TO FAITH

TEXT: JOHN 20:24-29

INTRO:
Fear says, “This is overwhelming, can I trust it?”
Doubt says, “I’m not convinced, prove it.”

OBJECTIVE:
To lead believers from doubt to a firm confession of faith in Christ.
