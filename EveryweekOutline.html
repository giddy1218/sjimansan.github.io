TITLE: A PSALM OF THANKSGIVING

TEXT: PSALM 100:1-5

INTRO:
Psalm – sacred songs and poems in the Bible.
Israel’s hymnbook, used in temple worship and personal devotion.
Expression of praise, worship, thanksgiving, and prayer.

OBJECTIVE:
To express our genuine gratitude through worship.

I - R _ _ _ _ _ _ G (100:1-2)

A) ENERGETIC (100:1) — “JOYFUL NOISE”
    (PSALM 32:11)

B) EARNEST (100:2)
    (COLOSSIANS 3:16-17)

II - R _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ P (100:3)

A) CREATED BY GOD (“it is He who made us”)
    (GENESIS 1:26; PSALM 139:14)

B) CHOSEN BY GOD (HIS PEOPLE)
    (EPHESIANS 1:4; 1 PETER 2:9)

C) CARED FOR BY GOD (SHEEP OF HIS PASTURE)
    (JOHN 10:14, 27-29)

III - R _ _ _ _ _ E (100:4-5)

A) CONTINUOUS WORSHIP (100:4)
    (HEBREWS 13:15-16; ROMANS 12:1)

B) CONFIDENT WITNESS (100:5)
    Confident — we rest securely in God’s unchanging character.
    Witness — we declare His faithfulness to all generations.
    thank, praise, bless His name.
    (LAMENTATIONS 3:22-23)
