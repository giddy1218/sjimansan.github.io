TITLE: CHRIST'S CHURCH

TEXT: MATTHEW 16:18-20

INTRO:
Religious people were looking for signs about the truthfulness of Christ—not to be enlightened but to influence people.
Christ asked His disciples who He is to the people.
(MATTHEW 16:13-16)

OBJECTIVE:
To understand how Christ established His church.

    Church — ekklesia (called-out ones)
    (1 PETER 2:9) (PHILEMON 1:2)
