TITLE: NEW COMMANDMENT

TEXT: JOHN 13:34-38

INTRO:
Old commandment — (LEVITICUS 19:18)
New — not new in existence, but new in: extent, expression, example, emphasis.

OBJECTIVE:
To understand the kind of love we must practice to Christ and to one another.
