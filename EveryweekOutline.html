TITLE: WHAT MAKES OUR NEW YEAR HAPPY?

TEXT: 2 CORINTHIANS 5:17-21

INTRO:
The passage teaches that true happiness flows not from outside circumstances, but from spiritual realities given by God.
Note: Proverbs 3:13 — wisdom and understanding.

OBJECTIVE:
To remind us of the biblical source of happiness.

I - R _ _ _ _ A _ _ _ _ _ N (5:17)
(Act of God by which He gives new life to a repenting sinner, making that person a new creation)

A) CHANGED CONDITION (“IN CHRIST”)
    (ROMANS 8:9) (TITUS 3:5)

B) CLEANSED PAST (“OLD…”)
    (1 CORINTHIANS 6:9-11)

II - R _ _ _ _ _ I _ _ _ _ _ _ N (5:18-19)
(God’s act of restoring sinners into a peaceful relationship with God through Christ)

A) DIVINE INITIATIVE (5:18)
    (ROMANS 5:1, 10)

B) DECLARED FORGIVENESS (5:19)
    (HEBREWS 10:17)

III - R _ _ _ _ _ E _ _ _ _ N (5:20-21)
(Believers’ calling and position to speak on behalf of Christ)

A) IDENTITY (5:20) — AMBASSADORS FOR CHRIST
    (MATTHEW 28:18-20)

B) IMPUTATION (5:21)
    (PHILIPPIANS 3:9)
