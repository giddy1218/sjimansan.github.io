TITLE: CHRIST'S HEART FOR MISSION

TEXT: MATTHEW 9:35-38

INTRO:
Mission – a specific purpose or assignment that must be fulfilled; it is God sending His people to reach others with the gospel.

OBJECTIVE:
To remind ourselves to continue the work of Christ.

I - CHRIST'S A _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ S (9:35)

A) He associated with people (WORDS)
    (LUKE 19:10, JOHN 1:14)

B) He aided their sickness (WORKS)
    (GALATIANS 6:10)

II - CHRIST'S A _ _ _ _ _ _ _ N (9:36)
Compassion – Christ felt their pain; love in action.

A) Because of their distress (fainted)
    (MATTHEW 11:28)

B) Because of their directionless lives (scattered, no shepherd)
    (ISAIAH 53:6)

III - CHRIST'S A _ _ _ _ L (9:37-38)

A) Problem (9:37)
    (JOHN 4:35)

B) Prayer and Participation (9:38)
    (ISAIAH 6:8)
