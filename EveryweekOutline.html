TITLE: FROM RECOGNITION TO RESPONSIBILITY

TEXT: MATTHEW 28:16-20

INTRO:
John chapters 20–21 show the third appearance of Christ.
Matthew 28 shows the final stage of how Christ commissioned His disciples.
Recognition of Christ leads to responsibility for Christ.

OBJECTIVE:
To show how we value the resurrection and the right response.

    (JOHN 9:38)

III - A _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ T OF THE DISCIPLES (28:19-20)

A) INSTRUCTION (28:19)
    Go. Make disciples. Baptize.
    (ROMANS 10:14-15) (ACTS 2:38)

B) IMPLEMENTATION (28:20)
    (COLOSSIANS 1:28)

C) INSPIRATION

CONCLUSION:
Right recognition of Christ produces reverent worship, submission to His authority, and faithful obedience to His mission.
