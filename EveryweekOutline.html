TITLE: TESTIMONY OF THE CHURCH

TEXT: 1 TIMOTHY 3:15-16

INTRO:
Universal / Local church.
Testimony — visible and verbal witness concerning God’s truth.
Context: Paul wrote to Timothy to teach how believers should behave in the house of God.

OBJECTIVE:
To understand the biblical identity and testimony of the church.

I - THE C _ _ _ _ _ _ T OF THE CHURCH (3:15a)
“...how thou oughtest to behave thyself in the house of God”

A) PROPER BEHAVIOR
    (1 PETER 1:15-16)

B) PRIVILEGED BELONGING (house of God)
    (EPHESIANS 2:19)

II - THE C _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ R OF THE CHURCH (3:15b)
“...church of the living God, pillar and ground of the truth...”

A) LIVING ASSEMBLY
    (2 CORINTHIANS 6:16)

B) LIFTING AUTHORITY (pillar... ground...)
    (EPHESIANS 4:14-15)

III - THE C _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ Y OF CHRIST (3:16)
(focus, most important)

A) REVEALED MYSTERY
    “Great is...”

B) REDEEMER MANIFESTED
    “God was...”
