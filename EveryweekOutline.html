TITLE: FROM NOTHING TO PLENTY

TEXT: JOHN 21:1-14
(3rd Appearance of Christ)

INTRO:
From Doubt to Faith — Thomas.
The passage shows how Christ meets their emptiness. It is not about fish and bread, but dependence, obedience, and restoration.

OBJECTIVE:
To remind us that human effort apart from Christ results in emptiness.
