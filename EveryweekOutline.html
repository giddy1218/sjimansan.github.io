TITLE: WRONG CHOICE

TEXT: MATTHEW 26:14-16

INTRO:
The passage shows that those who appear close to God can still make a wrong choice.

OBJECTIVE:
To guard us against choices that lead away from Christ.

I - THE A _ _ _ _ _ _ _ N (26:14)

A) PRIVILEGED POSITION (“one of the twelve”)
    (MATTHEW 10:1-4) (JOHN 6:70-71)
    (ROMANS 8:16-17)

B) PERVERTED PURPOSE (“went… to the chief priests”)
    (1 CORINTHIANS 15:33) (PSALM 1:1)

II - THE A _ _ _ _ _ _ _ T (26:15)

A) SELFISH BARGAIN (“What will you give me?”)
    (LUKE 12:15) (HEBREWS 13:5)

B) SHAMEFUL BENEFIT (silver)
    (EXODUS 21:32) (1 TIMOTHY 6:10)

III - THE A _ _ _ _ _ _ _ N (26:16)

A) A CALCULATED PLAN (“sought opportunity”)
    (PROVERBS 4:14-15)

B) A CORRUPT PATH (“to betray”)
    Betrayal — someone violates trust for personal gain.
    (HEBREWS 3:12)
