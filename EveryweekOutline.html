TITLE: DECISION-MAKING

TEXT: MATTHEW 2:12-21

INTRO:
Two contrasting responses:
    Faith that obeys God’s voice
    Fear that resists God’s will

OBJECTIVE:
To distinguish faith or fear in making decisions.

I - FAITH THAT D _ _ _ _ _ _ S GOD'S DIRECTION (2:12-15)

A) WARNING (2:12)
    (2 TIMOTHY 3:16) (PROVERBS 11:14)

B) WITHDRAWAL (2:13-15)
    (HEBREWS 11:8) (JOHN 10:27)

II - FEAR THAT D _ _ _ _ _ _ S HUMAN DECISION (2:16)
(PROVERBS 29:25) (2 TIMOTHY 1:7)

A) SINFUL ANGER
    (EPHESIANS 4:26-27)

B) SINFUL ACTION
    (1 JOHN 3:15)

III - FULFILLMENT THAT D _ _ _ _ _ _ S GOD'S DOMINION (2:17-21)

A) PROPHECY FULFILLED (2:17-18)
    (JEREMIAH 31:15)

B) PERFECT TIMING (2:19)
    (ECCLESIASTES 3:1)

C) PROVIDENCE (2:20-21)
    God’s wise and sovereign care and guidance over creation.
    God governs every step.
    (PROVERBS 37:23)
