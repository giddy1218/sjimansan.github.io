TITLE: NEW COVENANT

TEXT: LUKE 22:15-23

INTRO:
Covenant – God’s binding agreement with His people (Jews), establishing a relationship that includes conditions and promises.

OLD COVENANT – (JEREMIAH 31:31-34)
NEW COVENANT – (LUKE 22) — Gentiles are included.

OLD COVENANT        NEW COVENANT
Law                     Grace
External obedience     Internal change
Serve by guilt            Serve by gratitude
Repeated sacrifices    Once-for-all sacrifice

OBJECTIVE:
To value our relationship with Christ so that our service is not a burden but a blessing.
