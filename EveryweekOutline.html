TITLE: DEVELOPING YOUR SPIRITUAL GIFTS

TEXT: 1 TIMOTHY 4:1-16

INTRO:
Understanding (1 CORINTHIANS 12:1-7)
Discovering (ROMANS 12:3-8)
Gifts do not grow automatically. Just as natural abilities require training and practice, spiritual gifts require discipline, devotion, and development.

OBJECTIVE:
To remind us that serving with spiritual gift/s is a mark of growing.
