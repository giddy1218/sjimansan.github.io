TITLE: HOPE THAT UNITES

TEXT: ROMANS 15:1-7

INTRO:
People today seek satisfaction in relationships, success, and material things—yet like the Samaritan woman, many are still thirsty.
DO YOU KNOW?
1. Negative Experience Index
- Gallup Negative Experience Index rose from 23 (2007) to 33 (2021).
- Over 300 million people globally suffered from depression in 2015.

2. Live Evaluation Index
- “Gallup’s global survey shows that only about 29% of people consider themselves ‘thriving,’ while over 70% say they are either struggling or suffering."
- "This means most people today don’t see their lives as truly satisfying.”
OBJECTIVE:
To understand that true satisfaction and purpose are found not in worldly pursuits but through a PERSONAL RELATIONSHIP with Jesus Christ, who offers us "living water."

Conclusion/Application:
1. Identify Your True Need:
Examine yourself and find areas where you've sought satisfaction in temporary solutions. Only Jesus can truly fill that emptiness.

Despite any past mistakes or societal judgments, boldly share how Jesus changed your life.
