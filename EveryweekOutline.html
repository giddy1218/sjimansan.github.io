TITLE: A GODLY / SPIRITUAL FATHER

TEXT: 1 THESSALONIANS 2:10-13

INTRO:
Godly — a father whose life reflects God's character.
Spiritual — a father who shares, disciples, and instructs people in their walk in Christ.

Every spiritual father should be godly, but not every godly father functions as a spiritual father.
Note: (1 TIMOTHY 1:2)

OBJECTIVE:
To remind us that we should be both godly and spiritual.

I - HIS E _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ E (2:10)

A) CONFIRMATION ("Ye are witnesses...")
    (2 CORINTHIANS 3:2-3)

B) CHARACTER / CONDUCT
    Holily — during conversion
    Justly — righteous actions
    Unblameably — careful actions
    (MATTHEW 5:16)

II - HIS E _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ T (2:11-12)

A) INSTRUCTIONS (2:11)
    Exhorted — to keep on going right
    Comforted — do not lose heart
    Charged — see the value of obedience
    (COLOSSIANS 1:28)

B) INTENTION (2:12)
    (COLOSSIANS 1:10)

III - HIS E _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ S (2:13)

A) RECEPTION OF THE WORD OF GOD
    (ACTS 17:11)

B) RESULT OF THE WORD OF GOD
    (ISAIAH 55:11)
