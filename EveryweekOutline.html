TITLE: SADNESS IN FALSE NEWNESS

TEXT: ECCLESIASTES 1:8-11

INTRO:
What makes our New Year happy?
The book of Ecclesiastes was written to expose the emptiness of life lived “under the sun.”
Note: Ecclesiastes 1:2-4

OBJECTIVE:
To know why new things never fully satisfy the human heart and why there is sadness in it.

I - R _ _ _ _ _ _ _ S APPETITE (1:8)
(Human desire is never satisfied)

A) WEARINESS OF LIFE (“all things…”)
    (ISAIAH 55:2) (JOHN 6:27)

B) WANTING MORE (“eye… ear…”)
    (1 TIMOTHY 6:6-10)

II - R _ _ _ _ _ _ _ E ACTIVITY (1:9-10)
(Human life is a routine)

A) CYCLE WITHOUT CHANGE (1:9)
    (LAMENTATIONS 3:22-33)

B) COUNTERFEIT CLAIMS (1:10)
    (REVELATION 21:5)

III - R _ _ _ _ _ _ _ D ABSENCE (1:11)
(Human life and achievements are forgotten)

A) FORGOTTEN PEOPLE
    (PSALM 31:12) (HEBREWS 6:10)

B) FUTILITY OF LIFE
    (JAMES 4:14) (PHILIPPIANS 1:21)
