TITLE: THANKSGIVING THROUGH GIVING

TEXT: 2 CORINTHIANS 9:11-15

INTRO:
Action of expressing thanks — it is to be expressed in word, work, and worship.

OBJECTIVE:
To remind us that Christ is the ultimate reason for thanksgiving.
