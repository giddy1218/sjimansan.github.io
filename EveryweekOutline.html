TITLE: WHAT A MOMENT

TEXT: 1 JOHN 3:1-3

INTRO:
Moment – a short period of time; a God-given occasion.

OBJECTIVE:
To remind us of our identity in Christ and excite us to wait for the most awaited day.

I - A MOMENT OF M _ _ _ _ _ _ L (3:1)

A) LOVE OF GOD (BEHOLD)
    (JEREMIAH 31:3) (JOHN 1:29)
B) LEGITIMATE CHILD
    (MATTHEW 7:21-23) (ROMANS 8:9, 16-17)

II - A MOMENT OF M _ _ _ _ _ Y (3:2)

A) PROMISE OF CHRIST’S APPEARING
    (1 CORINTHIANS 15:51-52) (JOHN 14:3)
B) POWER OF HIS APPEARING
    (PHILIPPIANS 3:20-21)

III - A MOMENT OF M _ _ _ _ _ _ N (3:3)

A) HOPE
    (TITUS 2:13)
    H – ASSURANCE
    O – TRIALS
    P – CHANGE
    E – EXPECTATION

B) HOLINESS
    (1 CORINTHIANS 6:11) (1 PETER 1:15-16)
