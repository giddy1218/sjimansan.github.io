TITLE: GRACE GIVING

TEXT: 2 CORINTHIANS 8:1-7

INTRO:
O.T.- Biblical practice of giving(10%)of one's income as an obligation and act of worship to support GOD'S WORK, LEVITES, and the poor.
N.T.- Biblical practice as an act of worship, rooted in the idea that giving is motivated by the grace of God rather than obligation to support GOD'S WORK.
MATTHEW 23:23
Objective: To reconcile that O.T. and N.T. are both applicable and to express how the grace of GOD reflects our Christian life.
