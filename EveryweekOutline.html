TITLE: NEW WORLD FOR THE SAVED

TEXT: REVELATION 21:1-5

INTRODUCTION:
Genesis begins with the created world.
Revelation ends with the consummated world.

OBJECTIVE:
To look forward to the believers’ eternal home promised by God.
Note: Revelation 20:15
