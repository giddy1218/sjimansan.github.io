TITLE: NEW OUTLOOK, NEW LOOK

TEXT: EPHESIANS 4:20-24

INTRO:
Outlook – changed inside (way of thinking).
Look – changed outside (way of living).
Both should be experienced by those who are in God’s family.
Note: (2 TIMOTHY 3:5)

OBJECTIVE:
To recognize that true Christian transformation begins internally (mind, heart) before it shows externally (behavior and lifestyle).

    (COLOSSIANS 2:5-6)

B) PRACTICAL EDUCATION (4:21)
    (ROMANS 10:17) (JOHN 10:27)

II - R _ _ _ _ _ _ E the PAST (4:22)

    (COLOSSIANS 3:8-9) (1 THESSALONIANS 5:22)

    (HEBREWS 3:13)

III - R _ _ _ _ _ W the PURPOSE (4:23-24)

A) MENTAL RENEWAL (4:23)
    (ROMANS 12:1-2)

B) MORAL RESTORATION (4:24)
    (LUKE 1:74-75)

Cycle of Change
    (ROMANS 12:2) — Transform (Metamorphoo)
